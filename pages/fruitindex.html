Fruit Ninja

Fruit Ninja keeps you busy cutting the Fruits.
The more you cut the Fruits the score gets increased. Three lives are given at start of the game. Click Start button to start and play the game. Hover the mouse over the Fruits to slice. Do not loose the lives by missing the fruits Click Reset button to Restart the game.
